Health Info

Nutrition Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over processed foods
  • Limit sugary drinks and snacks

Exercise Tips

  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per day
  • Incorporate strength training and high-intensity interval training into your routine
  • Find activities you enjoy and make them a part of your daily routine

  • Type 2 diabetes and insulin resistance
  • Mental health and stress management
